Diligence. Collaboration. Results.

Marie is an emerging web devlopment student currently enrolled in the coding bootcamp at the University of Central Florida. Marie has integrated technology in the classroom throughout her career as an educator. She has taught in several Christian schools in Florida for the past 13 years.

Education is where her passion for technology began as it enriched her students' learning experiences and increased their engagement in course content. Technology in schools has set the stage for Marie as a web developer. In true teacher fashion, she loves to collaborate, learn, and get results. In the same way, Marie looks forward to collaboaring, learning, and achieving results in the tech industry as well.

Eventually, Marie will take coursework to prepare her for professional opportunities in cybersecurity. Stay tuned to find out how Marie's new career is developing as she explores the male-dominated tech industry for the first time in Spring of 2018...
